ALEXA BURAKOFF

Product Designer

Hi, I’m Alexa. I design engaging user experiences and go-to-market strategies for start ups with big ideas.

The word design means a lot to me - it infiltrates everything from the product specifications to the implementation strategy and the process that surrounds it. I am passionate about the experience of the users and helping the team achieve their best work. This belief has led me to excel in all facets of product development including experience design, visual design, iteration planning, project management, maintaining a high level roadmap and I've recently picked up html and css.

I am currently available for part time or full time hire in San Francisco, CA. My portfolio includes samples of work from my 6+ years of experience in all facets of product design - the user experience, the visual design and the strategy behind it. Please enjoy.
