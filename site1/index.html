ParkPlease

We make parking stress free.

ParkPlease launched it’s service in May 2012 for San Francisco’s Outside Lands Music Festival, held every year at Golden Gate Park. Our launch made city and regional headlines and helped over 180,000 concert goers find stress free parking. Since then, ParkPlease has connected thousands of parking space owners to drivers looking for spaces and has successfully facilitated parking for numerous events including America’s Cup, Fleet Week, SF Giants Games and the Hardly Strictly Blue Grass Music Festival. In two summer seasons of operation, ParkPlease has registered hundreds of parking spots in the San Francisco Bay Area and facilitated over $25K in revenue.

I was the Product Co-founder on a team of three, operating the business and running the product from June of 2012 through August of 2013. I was responsible for all facets of the product design (planning, strategy, UX, UI) from inception through launch and beyond.

Below is a brief tour of our accomplishments.

Listing is easy, flexible and free.

Spot Owners can share their spot and earn money while they’re not using it. Renting a spot just two days a week can yield an extra $350 a month - not too bad for doing nothing!

Find a perfect spot. Reserve seamlessly - at home or on the go.

Our map elegantly showcases nearby parking spot locations and details, making it easy for renters to find their perfect spot and reserve in just seconds. Renters can compare variables such as neighborhood, price range, location, amenities and available dates by searching or filtering on the map. Spot Owner profiles also feature seller information.

And best of all, it’s customizable for any event.

Easily brand the service for any event, anywhere. Create special event modes and branded landing pages! Easily drum up supply and bring the demand to you.
