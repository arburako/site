Dinamundo

INTERACTION + VISUAL DESIGN

Overview

The Dinamundo iPhone app is based on a simple yet powerful principle - to use smartphones to enable and enhance our personal connections. Our phones are intimate parts of lives, like it or not. Instead of letting them constantly distract us from the world around us, we can use them to engage with our surroundings at a deeper level. That's the core concept behind Dinamundo, a travel check in game that uses popular gaming strategies to bring people together in the real world and help them create a meaningful connection.

My Role

The project took place from January 2012 through May 2012. Under the direction of Founder & CEO, startup veteran Erik Blachford, I led all facets of the product development lifecycle - strategy, interaction + visual design, execution process, etc - from concept ideation through product launch.

Challenge

The Original App

Dinamundo started back in 2011 with the goal of connecting travelers and locals through a check in game. In it's first implementation, it was a pretty basic Facebook App where users could search for and message other members. They could then "check in together" through Facebook's existing check in feature.

I was first introduced to the product when a colleague was asked to be on call for product maintenance, to help fix any issues that might arise but not for new feature development. We were both inspired by the idea of using game mechanics to foster real world connections and impressed with the innovative thinking behind "checking into people." At the same time, we agreed that the shared "check in" experience was muddled by the limitations of the current implementation as a Facebook App.
