Welcome!

My name is Haraldur Thorleifsson. But you can call me Halli.
I am a Creative Director and designer of things that appear on screens.

I can name-drop if you want. I can tell you that I’ve worked for companies like Google, The Economist, Motorola, Asics, TiVo, Microsoft, YouTube and Square. And if you twist my arm I can also tell you about some awards I’ve won; Webbys, FWA, Awwwards - the usual suspects. But enough about me, how have you been?
