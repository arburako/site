My Work

Hi, I'm Alexa

I design engaging user experiences and go-to-market strategies for startups with big ideas. The word design means a lot to me - it infiltrates everything from the product specifications to the implementation strategy and the process that surrounds it. I am passionate about the experience of users and helping the team achieve their best work. This belief has led me to become proficient in all facets of product development including:

Research

Understand the business and competitive landscape. Learn how people interact with the brand and product. Identify strategic opportunities and design goals.

Design

Define and design the product. Combine smart interaction, interface, and visual design into a compelling product that resonates with the target audience.

Refine

Test concepts, designs, and prototypes with real users. Combine learnings with gut instinct and experience. Iterate and refine in pursuit of perfection.

Featured Projects

ParkPlease

In the summer of 2012, I co-founded ParkPlease, an online service allowing anyone to rent a space parking space to those who need to park. I led the product strategy, design and execution through a successful launch for the Outside Lands Music festival. Our launch made city and regional headlines and helped over 180,000 concert goers find stress free parking.
